Frequently Asked Questions

You can choose a safari package and contact us through our Contact page to complete your booking.

3. Which payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, MasterCard, PayPal, Bank Transfer, and M-Pesa payments.

4. Are your safari guides experienced?

Yes. Our guides are licensed professionals with years of experience in Tanzania's national parks.

5. What should I carry during a safari?

Bring comfortable clothes, sunglasses, sunscreen, a camera, and personal identification documents.
